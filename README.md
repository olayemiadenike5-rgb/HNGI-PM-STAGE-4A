# Finora (Smart Money Management System)

## What Finora Does
Finora is a smart money management system that helps users plan around expenses, track and prioritize their spending using real-time account data.  
Instead of budgeting based on guesses, Finora connects to your bank account and allow you organizes your monthly expenses(recurring & non recurring) by priority ensuring essentials come first.


## Why It Matters
Many people struggle to manage their finances effectively because traditional budgeting tools only track spending after it happens.  
Finora bridges that gap by showing you what you can afford before you spend  giving you clarity, control, and peace of mind.

With Finora, users can:
- Plan based on recurring expenses and real income, not assumptions.  
- Prioritize essential expenses automatically.  
- Get insights and reminders to stay on budget.  

## Who It’s For
Finora is built for:
- Young professionals trying to manage salaries and avoid end-of-month surprises.  
- Freelancers with irregular income who need clearer financial planning.  
- Students and early earners learning how to budget responsibly.  

##  Key Features
- Bank Account Integration – Connect your accounts securely to fetch real-time balances .  
- Expense Creation & Prioritization – Create and arranges expense by priority (e.g., rent before entertainment).  
- Spending Insights Dashboard– View income, expenses, and savings in one clean dashboard.  
- Budget Reminders & Nudges – Get smart notifications about how your spending is trending for the month.  

## Technologies 
- Frontend-React.js, Tailwind CSS  
- Backend-Node.js (Express)   
- API Integration - Mono or Paystack (for secure bank connection) 
- Hosting-Vercel (Frontend), Render or AWS or digital Ocean (Backend) 

## Vision
Finora aims to become your personal finance companion, a tool that doesn’t just track your money but guides you toward better financial habits.
